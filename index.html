Features

Architectural features define the design, structure, and functionality of a building. Architects consider aesthetics, durability, functionality, and environmental impact during the design process. Key features include balance, proportion, spatial arrangement, lighting, ventilation, and material selection. In modern architecture, technology plays a crucial role, incorporating smart buildings and eco-friendly materials. Traditional architecture reflects local culture and climate influences.
