Features

Architectural features define the design, structure, and functionality of a building. Architects consider aesthetics, durability, functionality, and environmental impact during the design process. Sustainable architecture reduces environmental impact. The Great Pyramid was the tallest structure for centuries. Japanese architecture emphasizes simplicity and nature.
